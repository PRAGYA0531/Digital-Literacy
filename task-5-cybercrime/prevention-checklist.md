**Stay Safe Online**

* Never share your OTP, PIN, or bank details with anyone.
* Do not accept unknown payment requests on UPI apps.
* Always verify the sender before making any transaction.
* Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages.
* Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
* Regularly check your bank account for unusual activity.
* Use only official and trusted apps for online payments.
* Be cautious of offers that seem too good to be true.





**Report Cybercrime**

* National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
