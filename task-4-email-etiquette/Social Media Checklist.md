**Social Media Checklist:**

**Do’s**

&#x20;•Think before posting any content online. 

&#x20;•Keep your profile professional and clean. 

&#x20;•Use privacy settings to control your information. 

&#x20;•Verify information before sharing it. 

&#x20;•Be respectful and polite in online interactions.

&#x20;•Be careful regarding any sensitive topic.



**Don’ts**

&#x20;•Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly. 

&#x20;•Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content. 

&#x20;•Do not engage in online arguments or harassment. 

&#x20;•Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages. 

&#x20;•Do not spread fake news or unverified information.

&#x20;•Do not hurt anyone's personal sentiments regarding sensitive topics.
